About Us

Skill Change App was created by a group of developers to increase the chance of get basic education about any skill.
It aims that anyone can give a training about his/her talent or have a training from an ametour just like you, in order to learn different skills as a hobby or even as a job!

Gain new skills from playing football to learning how to educate your puppy!

Skill Change App provides you to interact directly with your trainer or trainee; so that you can set up your course like ever you wanted!

It is all free to find a course about your new learning plans, request from trainer to give you a personlized course and, of course, the payment is will be up to you two!

  • Easy to decide about payments.
  • Safe, because it is up to your requests.
  • You can reach any kind of education, because it is worth it.

Product Features

FAST

You can message to a course trainer directly!

EXPERIMENTAL

Anything can be a course. Just think about it!

FOLLOWABLE

You can track your progress in each course easily!

INTERACTIVE

You can create a training programme or get involved one any time!

Everything can be a training!

Besides the basic skills such as drawing or hand-made stuffs, everything, including time management skills or how to make a pencil can be a training; if you have the desire of learn!

We are aware of everyone is talented but in different areas. We aimed to use that talents if anyone need that.

Doing basic math, playing football, dancing tango, informations about historic fashion, chemical reactions, creating wooden stuffs, drawing with crayons and etc. can be learned by anyone. So, if you have a talent in those, you can either give a training to your special students or be a one to learn that skills.

All process and progress are up to you!

You can schedule your improvement, if you want to handle the course fast, make it fast, or if you want to learn in a long time, track your process wider.

Interact with other students who take same courses with you. Discuss and enjoy your courses by helping each other.

Access from anywhere and do not drop your learning. Easy to follow from your mobile phone.

Free to offer & request!

Users are free to give payment for their course by discussing with each other, or they can offer an equal level of course.

Each skill should be evaluated by levels, and every level should have its own scores for users so that users can find appropriate courses for themselves.

Users who take/give courses and share their progresses in the application will be awarded periodically, so people will keep using the application regularly
